

Toon Bird Flock uses a vertex colored model bird it is low poly, uses no textures and comes with 3
animaton ready to fly and sit idle. The birds fly around in a flock that is easily customizable.


Features
• Vertex colored models * 
• Area roaming birds that moves in flock
• Easily customize amount, size, speed and roaming area
• 366 Tris 
• Includes demo scenes and shaders 
• Includes mesh particle system

* No textures or UV’s (vertex colored, uses little memory)
